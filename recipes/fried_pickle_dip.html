Fried Pickle Dip

Get fried pickle flavors with a fraction of the effort.

This 5-ingredient Fried Pickle Dip is guaranteed to add “zing” to any appetizer spread, and it comes together in no time.

This recipe incorporates pickle juice and crunchy chopped pickles to bring in full-force pickle flavor.
And, the topping of savory toasted breadcrumbs brings in that crunchy “fried” element that makes the appetizer so
Creamy, tangy, and full of texture.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup seasoned panko (Japanese-style breadcrumbs), toasted
  • 3/4 cup drained and chopped dill pickles, divided, plus 2 tablespoons dill pickle juice (from 1 [16 ounce] jar)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons chopped fresh dill
  • wavy potato chips(or any other chip), for serving, plus crushed chips for garnish

Steps

  1. Stir together cream cheese, sour cream, pickle juice, and
    1/2 cup of the pickles in a medium bowl until smooth and combined.

  2. Cover and chill dip at least 30 minutes or up to 3 hours.
    Bring to room temperature before serving, about 30 minutes.
    Transfer to a serving bowl and garnish with panko, dill, crushed potato chips, and remaining 1/4 cup pickles.

  3. Serve with potato chips.
