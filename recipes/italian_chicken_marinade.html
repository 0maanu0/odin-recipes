Italian Chicken Marinade

This Italian dressing chicken marinade is a super simple but delicious way to add flavor before grilling.

This recipe is not only relatively fast to prepare and cook, for four people, but its also cheap

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Salad dressing, garlic powder, and salt together in a shallow baking dish.
    Add chicken breasts and turn to coat.
    Cover the dish with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator, 4 hours to overnight.

  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.

  3. Remove chicken from marinade and shake off excess.
    Discard remaining marinade.
